Pothos Plant
Epipremnum aureum

Pothos plants have a spade shaped leaf and come in a variety of colors: dark green, bright lemon and lime-colored, green and white leaves. The leaves feel thick and waxy.

Pothos Care

Sunlight

These plants need low light to bright indirect light. Leaves will burn and dry out in bright direct light.

Water

Soil needs to completely dry out in between watering.

Fertilizer

Fertilize about once every three months.

Toxic to pets
Snake Plant
Sansevieria

Snake plants have long bladed-shaped leaves that stand straight in the air.

Snake Plant Care
Sunlight

These plants do really well in bright indirect light but can also grow in low light conditions.

Water

Soil needs to completely dry out in between watering.

Fertilizer

Fertilize every 4-6 weeks.

Toxic to pets
Chinese Money Plant
Pilea peperomioides

Chinese money plants have bright green, pancake-shaped leaves.

Chinese Money Plant Care
Sunlight

These plants thrive in bright indirect light.

Water

Soil needs to completely dry out in between watering. They also need well draining potting soil.

Fertilizer

Fertilize about once a month.

Monstera
Monstera

Monstera plants have large dark-green leaves with natural holes in them. In some species, the holes grow out to the edge.

Monstera Care
Sunlight

These guys do really well in bright to mediumm indirect light.

Water

Soil needs to completely dry out in between watering and they need well draining soil with perlite or lava rocks.

Fertilizer

Fertilize every 2 weeks to once a month during its growing season.

Peace Lily
Spathiphyllum

Peace lilies have shiny, long lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color.

Peace Lily Care
Sunlight

These plants do well in bright indirect light.

Water

Should ahve moist soil, water only when top 1 inch of soil is dry. Do not overwater.

Fertilizer

Fertilize once or twice a year.

Toxic to pets
Jade Plant
Crassula ovata

Jade plants have thick, oval shaped leaves that are dark green in color. They have woody stems and look like mini trees.

Jade Plant Care
Sunlight

Jade plants do well in full sun.

Water

Water only when top of soil is dry to the touch - do not overwater.

Fertilizer

Fertilize every 6 months.

Toxic to pets
ZZ Plant
Zamioculcas zamiifolia

ZZ plants have long wand-like stems with fleshy,oval-shaped leaves. This plant looks like plastic due to its waxy, shiny coating.

ZZ Plant Care
Sunlight

These plants need low to bright indirect light.

Water

Let soil dry out in between watering. A well draining pot and soil will benefit this plant.

Fertilizer

Fertilize once a month.

Toxic to pets
Rubber Tree
Ficus elastica

Rubber trees have thick, leathery leaves that are oval shaped and are attached to thick stems.

Rubber Tree Care
Sunlight

These guys need medium to bright indirect light.

Water

Let soil dry out in between watering, water about every 1-2 weeks.

Fertilizer

Fertilize every 2 weeks.

Toxic to pets
Birds Nest Fern
Asplenium nidus

Bird's nest fern have long crinkled or wavy leaves and are light green in color. They closely resemble banana leaves.

Birds Nest Fern Care
Sunlight

This plant thrives in filtered sun with moderate shade.

Water

Soil should be moist at all times and water only when top of soil is dry to the touch. Do not overwater. A well draining soil is recommended.

Fertilizer

Fertilize once a month.

Fiddle Leaf Fig
Ficus lyrata

Fiddle leaf fig plants have large elongated leaves that resemble a fiddle.

Fiddle Leaf Fig Care
Sunlight

This plant loves bright filtered, it's recommended to put this plant in front of a window.

Water

Water once a week or every 10 days - do not over water. Well draining soil is recommended.

Fertilizer

Fertilize with every watering.

Toxic to pets
Calathea
Calathea

Calatheas have large dark green leaves with scalloped edges and silver brushmarks. The underside of the leaves are a burgandy purple color.

Calathea Care
Sunlight

Calatheas do well in medium to bright indirect light but can also tolerate low indirect light.

Water

Water every 1-2 weeks and allow soil to dry out halfway in between watering.

Fertilizer

Fertilize every 4 weeks and only in the spring/summer.

Peperomia Watermelon Plant
Peperomia argyreia

The Peperomia watermelon plant have leaves that closely resemy a watermelon skin. The leaves are oval, heart-shaped and green with light silvery stripes.

Peperomia Watermelon Plant Care
Sunlight

These plants do well in medium to bright indirect light.

Water

Water only when top half of soil is dry.

Fertilizer

Fertilize once a month in the spring/summer.
